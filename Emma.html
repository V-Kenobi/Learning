Anyways, for those of you who aren't familiar with her...

Emma is the best activist we have ever come to know in this century. She has shaped us in unexpected ways, encouraged us to trascend and be our truest version. She says that in order to grow as human beings we need to first find our strengths and acknowledge our weaknesses, because without a clear image of ourselves we can never come to the understanding and ulterior transformation of society.

Emma is one of the best actresses in the movie industry. She uses her voice to shine some light and give insight of what is currently happening in the world along with making visible the problems that we continue to carry in our society even though we consider them solved, such as the current existence of gender disparity in payment, in the workplace and in culture, as well as the underestimation of femininity in general.
